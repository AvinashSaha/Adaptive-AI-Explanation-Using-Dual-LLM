AdaptiveAI Healthcare Assistant

A research prototype that delivers transparent, dual‑LLM health information with explicit verification and evidence‑linked explanations.

This web application is part of an academic study on AI‑assisted healthcare decision support. It uses two large language models: the first generates an initial answer and the second independently reviews, critiques, and refines that answer, highlighting uncertainties and citing peer‑reviewed sources.

No personal identifiers are collected. All interactions are anonymous and used solely for usability and user‑experience evaluation in this study.

Important: AdaptiveAI is a research tool and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for clinical decisions.
